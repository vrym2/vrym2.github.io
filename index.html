Storage Oil Tanks Detection, Classification and Storage estimation.

Geo-Spatial intelligence is a key element in the Earth Observation sector. High resolution satellite images provide invaluable insights into forest management, flood risk analysis and in many other aspects. By collaborating with University of Leicester's Planet Labs data in my MSc dissertation thesis, I managed to develop and adapt a ML solution to identify and classify Storage Oil Tanks and calculate the storage capacity (calculating work in progress) using Compute Vision (CV) and Remote Sensing techniques
